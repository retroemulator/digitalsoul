About

Hi, I'm Luca. Born in 1980, based in Turin, hooked on technology and its real-world applications for over 38 years.

By day, I work as a Functional SAP Analyst and IT Manager at Eurisko, with hands-on experience implementing projects across the Stellantis group and its subsidiaries — FIAT, Maserati, Leapmotor, and others.

Outside of SAP, I'm a web developer, a social media manager, a photographer, and a music dj and selector. I'm recognized for problem-solving and reverse engineering — I bring a proactive mindset and a commitment to high-quality standards in everything I do.

Greenfield projects are where I really light up — I'd rather start from a blank page than retrofit something old. Building from zero forces clarity, and it's where the real creative work happens.

AI is part of my daily practice, not a side curiosity. I use it across writing, code review, image creation and editing — and this very portfolio was designed and coded together with Anthropic's Claude, iteration after iteration. I treat AI as a partner that sharpens judgment, never replaces it.
